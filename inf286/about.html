About Me

Hey! I’m William, a 20-year-old sophomore majoring in Cybersecurity at Northern Kentucky University. I’ve always been passionate about technology and the digital world, which led me to this field.

In my free time, you’ll usually find me doing one of three things: playing video games like Valorant, working out at the gym, or enjoying a good soccer match. I also like reading now and then, especially when I want to slow down and recharge.

One thing I really enjoy is building websites with HTML. There’s something satisfying about writing clean code and seeing it come to life. It’s a hobby I’m proud of and one I’m always looking to improve.
